Rabbits' Interests

What do rabbits like to do?

  • Chewing and digging
  • Exploration and territoriality
  • Social interaction and petting

What food do rabbits like?

  1. Dark leafy greens
  2. Fruits
  3. Alfalfa with different flavors

What do rabbits not like?

  • Sudden and loud noise
  • Being hugged or held tightly
  • Boring and sedentary environment
