HOW TO MAKE TIRAMISU

Italian Tiramisu Recipe

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert made with coffee-soaked Savoiardi biscuits, creamy mascarpone cheese, and cocoa powder. This dessert requires no baking and is ready to serve after chilling in the refrigerator for a few hours.

Preparation process:

Ingredients:

  • Savoyard biscuits: 24 pieces.
  • Mascarpone cheese: 500 grams.
  • Heavy cream: 200 ml.
  • Sugar: 100 grams.
  • Espresso coffee: 1 cup.
  • Cocoa: as desired (for decoration).

How to prepare:

  1. Brew the espresso and let it cool completely

  2. Whisk the mascarpone cheese with the sugar in a large bowl until completely smooth.

  3. Whisk the cold cream separately until it forms, then add to the cheese mixture.

  4. Quickly dip the Savoiardi biscuits in the coffee (just 1-2 seconds).

  5. Cover the bottom of the dish with the biscuits.

  6. Pour a layer of the cheese and cream mixture.

  7. Repeat this layering.

  8. Finish with cocoa powder.

  9. Reserve in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours (preferably overnight).
